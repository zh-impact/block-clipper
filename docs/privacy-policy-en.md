# Block Clipper Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: March 11, 2026
**Version**: 1.0

---

## Overview

Block Clipper ("we," "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle, store, and protect your information. By using this extension, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

**In short**: Block Clipper is a **completely local** extension. All data is stored on your device. We do not access, collect, or transmit any of your data to external servers.

---

## 1. What Information We Collect

### 1.1 User-Provided Content

Block Clipper saves the following information to your local device **only when you actively choose to save**:

- **Clipped Content**: Text or page snippets you select to save
- **Metadata**:
  - Page title (URL)
  - Source webpage URL
  - Timestamp of the clip
  - Optional custom user-defined title
  - AI-generated title (if you use this feature)

### 1.2 Information We Don't Collect

Block Clipper **explicitly does NOT collect**:

- ❌ Personal identity information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ User behavior data
- ❌ Device fingerprint information
- ❌ Geolocation
- ❌ Cookies or similar tracking technologies
- ❌ Usage statistics or analytics data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Local Storage

All data is stored in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) and is used only for:

- **Content Management**: Saving your clipped text and metadata
- **Search and Filter**: Enabling you to quickly find saved content
- **Import/Export**: Supporting data export to JSON or Markdown format, or import from files
- **Interface Features**: Displaying your clips in the sidebar, options page, and popup

### 2.2 AI Title Generation (Optional Feature)

If you choose to use the AI title generation feature:

- This feature uses **Chrome's built-in AI model** (Chrome 128+)
- **All processing happens locally on your device**
- **Clipped content is never sent to any external server**
- The AI model is used solely to generate brief title summaries of clipped content

---

## 3. Data Sharing

### 3.1 We Don't Share Your Data

Block Clipper **explicitly commits**:

- ✅ We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties
- ✅ We do not share your data with any third parties
- ✅ We do not transmit data to any remote servers
- ✅ We do not use analytics services or tools

### 3.2 Data Export

You may choose to export your clip data to local files (JSON or Markdown format) at any time. This is your data, and you have complete control over how it's used.

---

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local Storage Security

Block Clipper uses the browser's IndexedDB API for local storage, which provides the same level of security isolation as websites:

- Data is stored only in your browser's profile
- Other extensions and websites cannot access Block Clipper's storage
- Data is protected by the browser's same-origin policy

### 4.2 Data Encryption

While local storage typically doesn't require encryption, we recommend users:

- Use full-disk encryption on your operating system (such as FileVault, BitLocker)
- Lock your computer when stepping away
- Sign out of your browser account on shared devices

---

## 5. User Control

### 5.1 Complete Control

You have **complete control** over your data:

- ✅ **View all clips**: Browse your clips in the sidebar or options page
- ✅ **Edit content**: Modify clip titles or content
- ✅ **Delete clips**: Delete any clip at any time
- ✅ **Batch delete**: Select and delete multiple clips at once
- ✅ **Export data**: Export all data to local files anytime
- ✅ **Clear data**: Clear extension data through browser settings
- ✅ **Uninstall extension**: Uninstalling deletes all stored data

### 5.2 Data Access

Only **you** can access your saved clip data. We (the developers) cannot access, view, or process any of the content you store.
